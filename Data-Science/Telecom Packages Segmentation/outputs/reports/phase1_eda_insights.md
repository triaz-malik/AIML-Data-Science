# Phase 1 - EDA Insights

- Customers analysed: **3,333**
- Overall churn rate: **14.5%**

## Business questions

- Heavy users (top 10% by total minutes, >= 708 min): 334 customers, avg charge $75.95, churn 47.3% vs 14.5% overall.
- Top payers (top 10% by charges, >= $72.99): churn 62.6%; day-minutes drive most of the bill (day charge corr with total charge = 0.88).
- Frequent care callers (>=4 calls): 267 customers, churn 51.7% - a major red flag vs 14.5% overall.
- Churn drivers: international-plan holders churn 42.4% vs 11.5% without; high day usage and >=4 service calls are the strongest churn signals.
